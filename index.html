About Me

Hello, I'm Sharon McDermott! I am originally from the sunny shores of Santa Monica, California; I ventured to the vibrant city of Las Vegas at 10 years old, which I've since called home. My journey into data science began with a profound desire to help others, which initially led me to pursue a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. There, I discovered the transformative power of understanding human behavior and the potential to influence positive change through insightful analysis.

Beyond my academic and professional life, I am an avid storyteller, a hobby that fills my home with notebooks brimming with narratives. This passion for storytelling transcends into my professional work in data science. I leverage narrative techniques to transform complex data sets into compelling, understandable stories that inform and drive strategic decisions. I approach each challenge with a blend of analytical rigor and creative flair.

I am dedicated to enhancing data security and supporting data-driven decision-making within federal agencies. I focus on solving problems and crafting data narratives that elucidate the why and how of policy impacts and organizational improvements to make informative decisions. By integrating my background in psychology with my skills in data science, I aim to foster environments where data informs and inspires action.

Philosphy

Welcome! As a seasoned Data Science graduate focusing on data management and analytics, I am dedicated to enhancing program efficiency and effectiveness within public sectors. My philosophy is rooted in using a utilitarian approach, focusing on maximizing community well-being through strategic data use in public sectors. This ethical philosophy guides my efforts toward transparency and integrity, aiming to deliver informed, ensuring compassionate data-driven decisions to enhance program efficiency and overall societal good. Data science is more than just a profession to me—it's a responsibility and critical instrument in modern governance and healthcare. Utilizing meticulous research and strategic analytics, I work to uncover patterns that lead to innovative policies and interventions, aiming to enhance individual and community lives.

Experience

With my background in data science and experience in research coordination, I have developed a strong skill set in managing and analyzing complex datasets. At Cleveland Clinic and Retina Consultants of Nevada, I collaborated with principal investigators and sponsors, ensuring compliance with research protocols while handling sensitive clinical data. I was responsible for patient screening, data collection, and specimen processing, which strengthened my ability to manage large data systems while maintaining data accuracy and integrity. This hands-on experience with healthcare data has honed my skills in research, data management, and drawing insights from clinical data, all essential for driving data-informed decisions.

Furthermore, my role as a Research Assistant at National University has been instrumental in supporting academics with grant pursuits and grant writing. This experience has provided me with a unique perspective on the integration of data analytics to enhance grant proposals, ensuring that research projects are well-supported by data-driven evidence and sound methodologies.

Moreover, my role as a Forensic Specialist at the Nevada Department of Health and Human Services reinforced my ability to apply data analytics in regulatory environments. This experience, paired with my technical skills and commitment to improving program efficiency, positions me to contribute effectively in high-impact roles such as a program analyst or data scientist within federal agencies. By leveraging data to inform policy and operational decisions, I am well-equipped to support federal initiatives that rely on data-driven insights for success.

In addition to my practical experience, my educational focus on Database Analytics within my Master's in Data Science has equipped me with technical expertise in SQL, Python, and machine learning. I’ve applied these skills in various data-driven projects, using statistical techniques to analyze both continuous and categorical data. My proficiency in designing databases and employing machine learning algorithms allows me to build and refine predictive models for optimizing decision-making processes. This combination of practical experience and technical knowledge enables me to tackle complex data challenges and develop solutions grounded in statistical rigor and advanced analytics.
